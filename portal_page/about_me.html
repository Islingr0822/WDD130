About me

Hey it's me! The creator of this page! I'm Tristan,
and this was for my WDD130 class. It's been a fun project.

I love to build computers and systems. I love to learn more about
code and development. I hope to make video games someday,
and enjoy exploring the many worlds they give us access to.
I love music too. I listen to it all the time, and like to make it too.
I can sing, play most percussion instruments, and am learning guitar
as best I can. A fun project I'm planning next for this class
is a page all about magikarp! I'm also planning a physical project
where I'm going to replace all the switches on my keyboard
with new ones! It'll be great soldering practice!
